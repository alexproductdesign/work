Work Playground About CV

I'm a Senior Product Designer with 8+ years of experience. Currently at Trusted Shops, Europe's leading e-commerce trust platform, where I design complex B2B products end-to-end – design systems, data-heavy tools and internal workflows that teams actually use.

I sit between research and design craft. I talk to users, run surveys, dig into product analytics – and only then open Figma. My favorite kind of problem is a vague one: when the data shows something is off, and interviews reveal why.

How I work

Evidence first, pixels second. I validate ideas through user testing and A/B experiments rather than opinions, and I like shipping small and learning fast. I care about the parts of B2B design that are easy to neglect – a coherent design system, clear workflows, and adoption – and I just finished Reforge's Product Growth program to sharpen the activation and retention side.
