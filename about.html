I'm a Senior Product Designer with 8+ years of experience. Currently at Trusted Shops, Europe's leading e-commerce trust platform, where I own design end-to-end – from discovery to delivery.

I sit between research and design craft. I talk to users, run surveys, dig into product analytics – and only then open Figma. My favorite kind of problem is a vague one: when the data shows something is off, and interviews reveal why.

Evidence first, pixels second. I validate ideas through user testing and A/B experiments, ship small and learn fast. I like complex problems where the win is a cleaner, measurable result.
